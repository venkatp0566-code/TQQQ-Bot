**TQQQ is a 3x leveraged ETF.** Leveraged ETFs are complex instruments that carry substantial risk:

- Leveraged ETFs can lose value extremely rapidly, including losing **all or more than the invested amount** in a short period
- Leveraged ETFs suffer from **volatility decay** (beta slippage), meaning they underperform their underlying index over the long term in volatile markets
- Leveraged ETFs are **not suitable for buy-and-hold investing** and are designed for short-term trading
